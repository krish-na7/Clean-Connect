About CleanConnect

Our Mission & Vision

CleanConnect is dedicated to transforming waste management and urban cleanliness through innovative technology and active community involvement. Our mission is to streamline the reporting and resolution of cleanliness issues, fostering collaboration between residents and local authorities. Our vision is to create a cleaner, greener city where technology bridges gaps and communities work together for a sustainable future.

How It Works

CleanConnect allows citizens to easily report cleanliness issues such as litter, overflowing bins, and illegal dumping through our intuitive web platform. Reports are sent directly to local authorities, who can then track, manage, and resolve the issues efficiently. Our system ensures transparency by providing real-time updates on the status of each report, fostering trust and collaboration between the community and local government.
